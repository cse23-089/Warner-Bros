About Us

Welcome to Warner Bros.

Warner Bros. Entertainment Inc., a global leader in all forms of entertainment and their related businesses, is at the forefront of every aspect of the entertainment industry. From feature films and television to home entertainment, animation, comic books, video games, and beyond, Warner Bros. sets the standard for creative excellence and technological innovation.

Our History

Founded in 1923 by Harry, Albert, Sam, and Jack Warner, Warner Bros. has played a pivotal role in shaping the entertainment landscape. With a rich legacy spanning over a century, we have been home to groundbreaking films, beloved television shows, and iconic characters that have become a part of popular culture.

What We Do

Film Production

Warner Bros. Pictures has produced some of the most celebrated films in cinema history. From timeless classics to modern blockbusters, our film division continues to deliver compelling storytelling, visionary directors, and unforgettable performances.

Television

Warner Bros. Television is responsible for many of the most acclaimed and popular TV shows. Our diverse slate includes drama, comedy, reality, and animation, appealing to audiences of all ages.

Home Entertainment

Warner Bros. Home Entertainment brings the best in entertainment to your home. Whether through digital downloads, Blu-ray, or streaming services, we ensure that you can enjoy our content anytime, anywhere.

Animation

Warner Bros. Animation has brought to life some of the most beloved characters and franchises. Our animated productions continue to captivate audiences with their creativity and innovation.

Video Games

Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment is a leading publisher of interactive content, producing popular games based on our extensive portfolio of intellectual properties. Our games offer immersive experiences across various platforms.

Our Vision

At Warner Bros., our vision is to be the most innovative, inspiring, and influential entertainment company in the world. We strive to tell stories that resonate, entertain, and inspire people across the globe. Our commitment to quality, creativity, and technological advancement drives us to push the boundaries of what’s possible in entertainment.

Social Responsibility

We believe in making a positive impact on society through our corporate social responsibility initiatives. Warner Bros. is committed to diversity and inclusion, environmental sustainability, and giving back to the communities we serve. Our initiatives aim to create a better, more inclusive world through the power of storytelling.
